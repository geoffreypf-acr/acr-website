Security built around how Range Rovers are stolen

Range Rover, Sport, Velar and Evoque models are repeatedly targeted by organised thieves using key cloning and relay attacks. ACR Automobile protects your Range Rover with a layered approach insurers recognise: a Thatcham Meta Trak S5 tracker for monitored recovery, paired with a vehicle immobiliser that stops the vehicle being driven away in the first place.

Recommended for your Range Rover

  • Meta Trak S5 — Thatcham S5 tracking with ADR driver tags and 24/7 recovery.
  • Vehicle immobiliser — Meta Trak S5 Deadlock, Scorpion X or Autowatch Ghost II, to defeat key cloning and relay theft.
  • Dash cameras — evidence-grade front & rear recording with parking mode.

Many Range Rover insurers now require S5 cover. We’ll confirm exactly what your policy needs and fit it discreetly at your home.

Questions

Frequently asked

Which tracker does my Range Rover need?
Most Range Rover policies require a Thatcham S5 tracker such as the Meta Trak S5. We’ll confirm your insurer’s exact requirement during your free assessment.
Should I add an immobiliser too?
Yes — we strongly recommend it. A vehicle immobiliser stops your Range Rover being driven away, while the tracker recovers it if moved. Together they’re the strongest protection.
Do you come to me?
Either works — whichever suits you. Bring your vehicle to our West London base (Holland Park, W11), or we'll come to you with mobile installation at your home, office or storage across London, Surrey and the Home Counties.
Will it affect my warranty?
No. We install to manufacturer standard, protecting your warranty.
