About the company

Company background

ACR Automobile is a London-based vehicle security and automotive concierge specialist serving owners of luxury, prestige and performance vehicles. The company provides insurance-approved Meta Trak tracker installations, immobilisers, dash cameras, Apple CarPlay upgrades and tailored vehicle protection across London and Surrey.

With a focus exclusively on prestige and performance vehicles, ACR Automobile is recognised for discreet, professional, insurance-compliant installation carried out by approved installers at the owner’s home or office.

Founder

Geoffrey Fernandez

GF

Geoffrey Fernandez is the founder of ACR Automobile and a recognised specialist in vehicle security and theft prevention for high-value vehicles. He has been featured in national and local press for his expertise in protecting prestige and performance cars against keyless and relay theft.

Under his leadership, ACR Automobile has built a reputation among London’s luxury vehicle owners for discreet, insurance-approved protection and exceptional customer service.
