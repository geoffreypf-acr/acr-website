Modern connectivity, factory finish

Depending on your vehicle's year and specification, Apple CarPlay and Android Auto can either be activated — where the hardware is already present but the feature is dormant — or fully retrofitted using your existing screen and controls. We confirm which option applies to your car before any work begins. Either way, the result uses your factory display, so maps, music, messages and calls appear exactly as they should — with no aftermarket look and no compromise to your interior.

What you get

  • Apple CarPlay & Android Auto can be activated or retrofitted, depending on your vehicle’s year.
  • Split-screen Apple CarPlay / Android Auto can be set to fullscreen.
  • Wireless Apple CarPlay & Android Auto on your factory display.
  • Retains original steering-wheel and rotary controls.
  • Reversing-camera and front-camera integration where supported.
  • Clean, reversible installation by a specialist engineer.

Questions

Frequently asked

Do you retrofit CarPlay for BMW, Mercedes, Audi and Porsche?
Yes — we retrofit wireless Apple CarPlay and Android Auto into BMW iDrive (CIC, NBT, EVO), Mercedes COMAND & MBUX, Audi MMI, Volkswagen MIB, Porsche PCM and Bentley, plus McLaren, Aston Martin and Ferrari. We integrate with your factory screen and controls for an OEM-clean result.
Will it look aftermarket?
No. We integrate with your factory screen and controls so the result looks and feels original — simply with CarPlay and Android Auto added.
Is the installation reversible?
Yes. Our retrofits are clean and reversible, with no permanent changes to your vehicle’s wiring.
Which cars can you upgrade?
Most prestige models from the last decade that have a factory screen. Tell us your make, model and year and we’ll confirm compatibility.
Can you keep my reversing camera?
In most cases yes — we retain and integrate existing reversing and front cameras where the system supports it.
