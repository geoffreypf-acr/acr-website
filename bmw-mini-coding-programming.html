Questions

Frequently asked

What can you code or retrofit?
A wide range — CarPlay activation, video-in-motion, digital cluster retrofits, comfort features and more. Tell us your vehicle and we’ll confirm what’s possible.
Do you register replacement batteries?
Yes. We carry out battery registration and coding so your BMW or MINI recognises a new battery and charges it correctly.
Is coding safe for my car?
Done correctly, with the right equipment, yes. We work within your vehicle’s capabilities and can reverse changes if you wish.
Which systems do you cover?
CIC, NBT, NBT EVO and MGU on BMW, and the equivalent MINI systems.
