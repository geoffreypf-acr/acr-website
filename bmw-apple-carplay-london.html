BMW CarPlay, integrated into iDrive

If your BMW predates wireless smartphone integration, an ACR retrofit adds Apple CarPlay and Android Auto to your iDrive (CIC, NBT and EVO generations).   • Apple CarPlay & Android Auto can be activated or retrofitted, depending on your vehicle’s year.

  • Reversing and 360° cameras retained and integrated where supported.

Questions

BMW CarPlay — frequently asked

Can you add Apple CarPlay to my BMW?
Yes. We retrofit wireless Apple CarPlay and Android Auto into BMW iDrive (CIC, NBT and EVO generations), using your factory screen and iDrive rotary controller so it looks and works like original equipment.
Will my BMW still look factory?
Yes. The integration uses your existing iDrive display and controls — there is no aftermarket head unit and no change to your interior.
Is it wireless?
Yes — wireless Apple CarPlay, plus Android Auto where the BMW system supports it. Your phone connects automatically once paired.
Is the installation reversible and mobile?
Our BMW CarPlay retrofits are clean and fully reversible, with no permanent wiring changes. We fit at our West London (W11) base or mobile across London and Surrey.
