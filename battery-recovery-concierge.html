Questions

Frequently asked

Can you jump start modern vehicles?
Yes. We carry professional-grade equipment suited to modern prestige and performance vehicles, including those with stop/start systems and sensitive electronics where a careless boost can cause damage. We diagnose first, then start safely.
Do you provide battery replacement?
Yes. We supply and fit the correct OEM-specification battery for your vehicle, and where required we register and code the new battery to the car so the charging and energy-management systems work correctly.
Can you code BMW and Mercedes batteries?
Yes. Many modern BMW, Mercedes-Benz and other premium vehicles require the new battery to be registered/coded to the car. We carry out battery coding and registration as standard so your vehicle recognises the new battery.
Do you arrange vehicle recovery?
Yes. Where a battery or jump start isn’t enough, we coordinate recovery and transportation of your vehicle to a trusted destination — your home, a specialist, or our facility.
Can you provide a replacement vehicle?
In many cases, yes. We can arrange a replacement vehicle while yours is being recovered or repaired, subject to availability. Tell us your requirements when you enquire.
What areas do you cover?
We operate across London and into Surrey and the Home Counties — including Kensington, Chelsea, Knightsbridge, Mayfair, Richmond, Cobham, Weybridge, Esher and beyond.
Do you offer emergency call-outs?
We aim to respond rapidly and, wherever possible, the same day. Contact us by phone or WhatsApp with your location and vehicle details and we’ll tell you the earliest we can reach you.
