We fit premium THINKWARE systems — from the 2K Q200 to the flagship 4K U3000 PRO — hard-wired so there are no trailing cables, with the camera bodies concealed behind the mirror and trim for a factory-clean finish.

What we install

  • Front & rear 4K recording with wide dynamic range for night clarity.

Questions

Frequently asked

Will the installation damage my interior?
No. Our engineers conceal cabling within existing trim and mount cameras discreetly, leaving a factory-clean finish with no drilling or visible wires.
Does parking mode drain my battery?
No. We fit a battery-protection kit that powers the camera safely and cuts off before your vehicle battery is affected.
Can you fit front and rear on any car?
We install front and rear systems on virtually all prestige and performance vehicles. We’ll confirm the best system for your model during your assessment.
Do dash cameras reduce insurance premiums?
Some insurers offer a discount for fitted dash cameras and they provide valuable evidence in a claim. We can advise on suitable approved systems.
