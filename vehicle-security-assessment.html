Questions

Frequently asked

How much does a tracker cost?
Pricing depends on the system your vehicle and insurer require — typically a Meta Trak S7 or S5, with or without immobilisation. We give you a fixed, transparent price during your assessment with no obligation.
What happens after I submit?
A security specialist — not a call centre — calls you back, usually the same day during business hours, to confirm your recommendation, price and the earliest mobile fitting slot.
Where are you based?
We’re based in West London and carry out mobile installations — we come to your home, office or storage across London, Surrey and the Home Counties.
Do you come to me?
Either works — whichever suits you. Bring your vehicle to our West London base (Holland Park, W11), or we'll come to you with mobile installation at your home, office or storage across London, Surrey and the Home Counties.
Which products do you recommend?
We recommend Meta Trak solutions — S7 and S5 trackers, immobilisers and combined packages — for their insurance approval, driver tags, app control and tracking. We’ll match the right one to your vehicle and policy.
