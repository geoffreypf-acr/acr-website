Questions

Frequently asked

Which BMW iDrive systems do you work on?
We cover CIC, NBT, NBT EVO and the latest MGU systems, across iDrive 5, 6, 7 and 8 generations — plus MINI NBT, NBT EVO, MINI Connected and Navigation Professional.
Can you fix a frozen or black iDrive screen?
In most cases, yes. Many frozen, black-screen or boot-looping faults are resolved by a professional reboot or software recovery without replacing the unit. We diagnose first and tell you the options.
Do you come to me?
Most BMW and MINI work is carried out mobile at your home or office across London and Surrey. Some repairs may need bench time — we’ll let you know when you enquire.
Can you retrofit or code features?
Yes. We carry out coding, retrofits and feature programming with dealer-level equipment. Tell us what you’d like and we’ll confirm what’s possible for your vehicle.
